# Sahayak AI - Demo Script (2-4 minutes)

## Opening (15 seconds)
"Imagine you're a first-generation college student trying to apply for a government scholarship. The form is 12 pages of legal language. You don't know if you're eligible, which documents you need, or where to start. Sahayak AI turns that confusion into a clear action plan."

## Step 1: Landing Page (15 seconds)
- Show the landing page with value proposition
- Click "Try Demo"
- "Sahayak is an AI copilot for forms and paperwork."

## Step 2: Demo Mode (20 seconds)
- Click "Load Demo & Start"
- "We've pre-loaded three realistic documents: a government scholarship, a medical reimbursement form, and a college admission application."
- Show the seeded profile: Priya Sharma, SC category, 82% in 12th, Rs.1.8L family income.

## Step 3: Document Overview (30 seconds)
- Show the parsed scholarship document
- Point out: title, authority, deadline, eligibility criteria, required documents, risk flags
- "Sahayak read the entire document and extracted every important detail."

## Step 4: Eligibility Verdict (30 seconds)
- Show eligibility page
- "Priya meets most criteria. She's SC, income is under Rs.2.5L, she has 82% marks. The engine explains exactly which criteria pass and which need review."

## Step 5: Checklist (20 seconds)
- Show missing documents checklist
- "Sahayak generated a prioritized checklist. Caste certificate and income certificate are missing. It tells you why each is needed."

## Step 6: Ask Sahayak (30 seconds)
- Ask: "Am I eligible?"
- Show the grounded response with citations
- "Every answer cites the specific document section. No hallucinations."

## Step 7: Final Readiness (20 seconds)
- Show readiness score breakdown
- "Clear score, clear next steps, clear deadline awareness."

## Closing (15 seconds)
"Sahayak AI is AI-native, not a chatbot with AI sprinkled on top. It solves a real problem for millions of students dealing with confusing paperwork. Thank you."

## Fallback (if parsing is slow)
- Use demo mode (pre-loaded data)
- Skip to eligibility and chat
- "The demo uses pre-parsed documents to show the full flow instantly."
