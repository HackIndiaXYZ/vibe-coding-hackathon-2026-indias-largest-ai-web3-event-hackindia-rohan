# Sahayak AI - Pitch

## One-Line Pitch
"Sahayak AI turns confusing forms and official documents into a clear checklist, guided application flow, grounded AI answers, and a ready-to-submit package."

## Problem
Millions of students, parents, and workers in India face confusing forms for scholarships, admissions, reimbursements, and government schemes. The forms are in legal language, requirements are buried in paragraphs, deadlines are unclear, and missing one document can mean rejection. Most people either give up or make costly mistakes.

## Solution
Sahayak AI is an AI copilot that:
1. Parses any form into structured, readable data
2. Checks your eligibility with clear reasoning
3. Auto-drafts form answers from your profile
4. Generates a prioritized document checklist
5. Answers grounded questions with document citations
6. Creates a submission-ready action plan

## Why Now
- AI capabilities (structured extraction, grounded chat) have reached the quality needed for this
- India's digital governance push means more forms are going online
- Students and workers need intelligent tools, not just form viewers

## Startup Potential
- **Market**: EdTech + GovTech intersection. Hundreds of millions of form submissions annually.
- **Monetization**: Freemium model. Free for basic use, premium for multi-form packs, agent-assisted filing.
- **Expansion**: Insurance claims, tax filing, visa applications, legal documents.
- **Moat**: Domain-specific extraction models, user profile portability, document templates.

## Why Judges Should Care
- **AI-Native**: AI is the core product, not a feature bolted on
- **Real Problem**: Solves actual pain for real users
- **Demo-Ready**: Full working flow in under 3 minutes
- **Startup-Ready**: Clear path to revenue and scale
- **Cross-Category**: Scores on AI Native Apps, Startup Prototype, and Student Innovation
